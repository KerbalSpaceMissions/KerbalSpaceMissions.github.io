Welcome to Mission Infinity

Explore Boundless Horizons with Ever-evolving Missions.

Mission Infinity is a hub for all enthusiasts of Kerbal Space Program (KSP), dedicated to providing an endless stream of innovative and abstract missions to challenge and inspire players. Whether you're a seasoned KSP veteran or just beginning your journey into the cosmos, Mission Infinity offers a platform where imagination knows no bounds.

Our mission is simple: to ignite creativity and exploration within the KSP community by presenting new and abstract mission scenarios that push the limits of what's possible in the game. From daring interplanetary expeditions to mind-bending scientific experiments, each mission is crafted to provide a unique and engaging experience for players of all skill levels.

At Mission Infinity, the adventure never ends. Our team of mission designers is constantly working to create fresh content, ensuring that there's always something exciting to discover. Whether you're looking to test your piloting skills, flex your engineering muscles, or simply marvel at the wonders of the universe, you'll find it all here.

Join us on a journey beyond the stars, where the only limit is your imagination. Browse our ever-expanding library of missions, share your own creations, and connect with fellow spacefarers from around the world. Together, let's push the boundaries of space exploration and embark on an infinite voyage of discovery in Kerbal Space Program.
